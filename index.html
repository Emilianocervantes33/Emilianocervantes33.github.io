The history of the Tamal

The history of the tamal goes back all the way to 8000 and 5000 BC.It is believed to have originated from Guatemala and Mexico I absolutly love them and my mom and grandma make them all the time.

The tamales started then spreading to the rest of Latin America Its a corn based dough micture which you can fill up with some meat and cheese

Ingrediants for tamales

  • Corned based dough mixture
  • you could also fill it up with some meat and cheese
  • Broth, Beef chiken or vegatable will work
  • baking powder
  • salt
  • Cumin
  • Lard
  • dried corn husk

Instructions on how to create tamales

  1. Soak the corn husk
  2. Prepared desired filling
  3. Make the masa dough
  4. Assemble the tamales
  5. Tie the tamal(Optional)
